Code Chef's Seafood

Seafood Menu

  • Baked salmon with fennel & tomatoes

    A flavorful and eye-catching dish, featuring tender salmon fillets baked to perfection with aromatic fennel and juicy cherry tomatoes. A wholesome option that pairs beautifully with a side of steamed vegetables, quinoa, or crusty bread.

  • Cajun spiced fish tacos

    A taco filled with fish that has been seasoned with a bold, spicy Cajun spice blend, typically featuring flavors like cayenne pepper, garlic, and paprika, often served on a warm tortilla with toppings like crunchy cabbage slaw, a creamy remoulade, and a zesty salsa, creating a delicious combination of heat and tanginess.

  • Escovitch Fish

    Crispy fried fish, topped with a tangy, spicy pickled sauce made with vinegar, onions, carrots, bell peppers, scotch bonnet peppers, and allspice.

  • Fish fofos

    A flavorful seafood dish often prepared using fresh fish as the main ingredient, combined with rich, aromatic spices.

  • Fish pie

    A comforting and hearty dish made with a creamy filling of flaked fish, often a mix of white fish and smoked fish.

  • Fish Soup (Ukha)

    A traditional Russian soup made with fresh fish and a light, flavorful broth.

  • Fish Stew with Rouille

    Fish Stew with Rouille is a hearty and elegant dish, perfect for showcasing fresh seafood and rich Mediterranean flavors.

  • Garides Saganaki

    A traditional Greek dish featuring succulent shrimp cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce with aromatic herbs and spices.

  • Grilled Portuguese sardines

    A beloved dish deeply rooted in Portuguese culinary tradition. This simple yet flavorful preparation features fresh sardines seasoned with sea salt, then grilled over an open flame, which imparts a smoky, charred flavor to the fish.

  • Honey Teriyaki Salmon

    A deliciously sweet and savory dish that combines tender, flaky salmon with a glossy teriyaki glaze. A versatile recipe that pairs well with steamed rice, sautéed vegetables, or a crisp green salad.

  • Kedgeree

    A comforting and aromatic dish that combines flaked smoked fish, usually haddock, with rice, boiled eggs, and a blend of spices.

  • Kung Po Prawns

    a vibrant and flavorful Chinese dish that originates from the Sichuan province, known for its bold, spicy, and slightly sweet flavors.

  • Laksa King Prawn Noodles

    A spicy, fragrant noodle soup found across Southeast Asia, particularly Singapore, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

  • Mediterranean Pasta Salad

    A fresh, vibrant dish that captures the flavors of the Mediterranean region.

  • Mee goreng mamak

    A flavorful and spicy stir-fried noodle dish that hails from the Malaysian Indian Muslim community, commonly known as Mamak. This street food favorite is a harmonious blend of sweet, savory, and tangy flavors with a hint of spice.

  • Nasi lemak

    A beloved Malaysian dish, often considered the country’s national dish, celebrated for its rich and aromatic flavors.

  • Portuguese fish stew (Caldeirada de peixe)

    A traditional Portuguese fish stew, celebrated for its rich, hearty flavors and fresh ingredients.

  • Recheado Masala Fish

    A flavorful and aromatic dish hailing from the coastal regions of India, particularly Goa. This dish is a perfect combination of heat, acidity, and robust flavors, often served with steamed rice, flatbreads, or a side of salad to balance the intensity.

  • Salmon Avocado Salad

    A fresh and light, nutritious dish that combines the richness of fresh salmon with the creamy texture of avocado, creating a delightful contrast in both flavor and texture.

  • Salmon Prawn Risotto

    A luxurious and flavorful dish that combines creamy, comforting risotto with the rich tastes of fresh salmon and succulent prawns.

  • Saltfish and Ackee

    A Jamaican national dish and a beloved staple in Caribbean cuisine. The dish features salted codfish (saltfish) paired with ackee, a tropical fruit that is native to West Africa but has become an essential ingredient in Jamaican cooking.

  • Seafood fideuà

    A traditional Spanish dish originating from the Valencian region, similar to paella but made with short, thin noodles (fideuà) instead of rice. This dish is a celebration of fresh seafood and Mediterranean flavors, cooked in a wide, shallow pan for even cooking and presentation.

  • Shrimp Chow Fun

    A classic Cantonese stir-fried noodle dish that features tender shrimp and wide, flat rice noodles known as ho fun. The dish is renowned for its smoky, slightly charred flavor, often achieved through a high-heat wok cooking technique called wok hei.

  • Sledz w Oleju (Polish Herrings)

    A traditional Polish dish that showcases marinated herring fillets preserved in a flavorful mixture of oil and aromatic seasonings.

  • Spring onion and prawn empanadas

    A delightful variation of the classic Latin American pastry. These golden, crispy turnovers are filled with a savory mixture of tender prawns and aromatic spring onions, creating a delicious blend of flavors in each bite.

  • Sushi

    A Japanese dish that consists of vinegared rice accompanied by various ingredients such as raw or cooked fish, seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.

  • Three Fish Pie

    A rich and hearty British dish that combines a medley of three types of fish, typically white fish like cod, haddock, and salmon, all baked together in a creamy sauce and topped with a layer of mashed potatoes.

  • Tuna and Egg Briks

    A brik is a thin pastry, similar to phyllo or spring roll wrappers, that is filled with a delicious combination of tuna, egg, and seasonings, then fried until golden and crispy.

  • Tuna Nicoise

    A classic French salad that hails from the coastal region of Nice, offering a vibrant and balanced combination of flavors.
